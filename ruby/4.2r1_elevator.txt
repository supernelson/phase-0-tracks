- Determine what floor you are currently on
- Determine what floor you want to go to
  - if the current floor is a number lower than your destination floor press "up".
  - if the current floor is a number greater than your destination floor press "down".
- Wait until the doors open then walk inside and face towards the doors.
- Look for the floor display box which will show a number of the current floor the elevator is on.
- While the elevator moves whistle a chipper song.
- When the display number matches your destination floor wait for the doors to open and get out.
- You have arrived!
